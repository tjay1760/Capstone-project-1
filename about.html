SHAKY'S ANNUAL COOKOUT 2023

Shaky's Cookout is an annual event that brings together people from various industries to network, socialize, and enjoy delicious food. It provides an opportunity for professionals to come together, exchange ideas, and build relationships in a relaxed and fun environment. With a menu featuring mouth-watering grilled foods and refreshing drinks, attendees can indulge in delicious cuisine while connecting with like-minded individuals. The cookout is held in a spacious outdoor venue, creating a festive atmosphere and allowing plenty of room for mingling, games, and entertainment. It has become a highly anticipated event in the community and a great way to bring together people from different backgrounds and industries for a day of celebration and connection.

For further information about the cookout contact us at shakysconference2023@shays.co.ke
